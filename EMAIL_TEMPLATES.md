[Resort Name]

Hi **[Guest Name]**,

Thank you for your reservation! You have successfully booked **[Room Name]**.

📋 **Reservation Details**
- **Booking ID:** BK-9C4K2Z
- **Room:** [Room Name]
- **Check-in:** [Date]
- **Check-out:** [Date]
- **Guests:** 4

💰 **Payment Summary**
- **Total Amount:** ₱5,000
- **Required Deposit:** ₱2,500
- **Balance Due:** ₱2,500

**[Resort Name] Team**
