# 🎬 Staycation Tutorial — Full Video Script

> **Total estimated length:** 8–12 minutes  
> **Format:** Screen recording + your voiceover  
> **Tool:** Loom (free) or OBS Studio (free)  
> **Tip:** Read each voiceover line naturally. Don't rush. Pause between sections.

---

## 🎤 Before You Record

### Setup Checklist
- [ ] Open your live website (or localhost) in Chrome
- [ ] Make sure you have **at least 1 room** added with images
- [ ] Have your **GCash/Bank QR code** image ready
- [ ] Close unnecessary tabs (keep it clean)
- [ ] Set your browser to **full screen** (press F11)
- [ ] If using Loom: enable webcam in the corner (optional, adds a personal touch)

### Recording Tips
- Speak slowly and clearly
- Move your mouse smoothly (don't click too fast)
- Pause 1–2 seconds after each click so viewers can follow
- If you make a mistake, just pause and redo — you can trim later

---

## PART 1: INTRO (30 seconds)

### 📺 Show: Your website homepage

**🎤 Say:**
> "Hey! Welcome to your Staycation Booking Website. In this tutorial, I'm going to walk you through everything you need to know — from how your guests book, to how you manage everything from the admin dashboard. Let's get started!"

---

## PART 2: THE GUEST EXPERIENCE (2–3 minutes)

> *Explain this first so your client understands what their CUSTOMERS will see.*

### Scene 2A: The Homepage

**📺 Show:** Scroll slowly through the homepage  
**🎤 Say:**
> "So this is what your guests see when they visit your website. They'll see your hero section at the top with your property name and tagline. As they scroll down, they'll see your available rooms, an about section, and your contact information."

**📺 Action:** Scroll to the Rooms section slowly

**🎤 Say:**
> "Here are your rooms. Each room shows the photo, name, price per night, and a 'Book Now' button. Your guests can browse through these and pick the one they like."

---

### Scene 2B: Booking a Room

**📺 Action:** Click "Book Now" on any room  
**🎤 Say:**
> "When a guest clicks 'Book Now', this booking window pops up. On the left, they can see the room photos, description, and amenities. On the right is where they select their dates."

**📺 Action:** Click a check-in date on the calendar  
**🎤 Say:**
> "They click their check-in date first..."

**📺 Action:** Click a check-out date  
**🎤 Say:**
> "...then their check-out date. The system automatically calculates the total price based on the number of nights."

**📺 Action:** Point to the price at the bottom  
**🎤 Say:**
> "See? The total price appears right here at the bottom. It always stays visible so the guest knows exactly what they'll pay."

---

### Scene 2C: Day Use Booking (if applicable)

**📺 Action:** Close the modal, open a room that has Day Use pricing  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Now here's a cool feature — if you set a Day Use price for a room, guests can book it for just the daytime. Watch this..."

**📺 Action:** Click only ONE date on the calendar  
**🎤 Say:**
> "When the guest clicks just one date, the system automatically detects it as a Day Use booking and shows the Day Use price instead. No need to toggle anything — it just works!"

---

### Scene 2D: Filling In Details

**📺 Action:** Fill in the guest form (Name, Email, Phone, Guests)  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Next, the guest fills in their details — their full name, email, phone number, and how many guests. They can also set their preferred check-in and check-out times."

**📺 Action:** Click "Continue"  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Once they click Continue, they move to the payment step."

---

### Scene 2E: Payment

**📺 Action:** Show the payment screen with QR codes  
**🎤 Say:**
> "On this screen, the guest will see your payment QR codes — for GCash, bank transfer, or whatever you've set up. They can choose to pay just the deposit or the full amount."

**📺 Action:** Point to the QR code and the payment options  
**🎤 Say:**
> "They scan the QR code with their phone, send the payment, and then click 'Confirm Booking'. That's it from the guest's side! They'll get a booking reference number, and you'll receive the booking in your admin dashboard."

**📺 Action:** Click "Confirm Booking" to show the success screen  
**🎤 Say:**
> "And they get this confirmation screen with their booking reference."

---

## PART 3: THE ADMIN DASHBOARD (3–4 minutes)

### Scene 3A: Accessing the Admin

**📺 Action:** Click "Admin" in the navbar  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Now let's look at YOUR side — the Admin Dashboard. Click 'Admin' in the navigation bar."

**📺 Action:** Enter the admin passcode  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Enter your admin passcode to get in. Only you and your authorized staff should have this code."

---

### Scene 3B: Calendar View

**📺 Action:** Show the Calendar tab  
**🎤 Say:**
> "The first thing you'll see is the Calendar view. This is your command center. Every booking shows up here, color-coded by status — yellow for pending, green for confirmed, and red for cancelled."

**📺 Action:** Click on a booking  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Click on any booking to see the full details — guest name, contact info, dates, payment status, and the amount. From here you can confirm, edit, or cancel the booking."

---

### Scene 3C: Managing Bookings

**📺 Action:** Show the confirm/cancel buttons  
**🎤 Say:**
> "When a new booking comes in, it'll be marked as 'Pending'. After you verify that the guest has sent their payment — they'll usually send you a screenshot on Messenger — you can click 'Confirm' to approve it."

**📺 Action:** Show the Overview/Stats tab  
**🎤 Say:**
> "You can also check the Overview tab to see your business stats — total bookings, revenue, and occupancy rates."

---

### Scene 3D: Room Management

**📺 Action:** Click on the Rooms tab  
**🎤 Say:**
> "The Rooms tab is where you manage all your rooms. Let me show you how to add a new one."

**📺 Action:** Click "+ Add Room"  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Click 'Add Room'... then fill in the details."

**📺 Action:** Fill in the form slowly  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Give it a name... add a description... set the price per night. If you want to offer day use, set a Day Use Price here — this is optional."

**📺 Action:** Show capacity, image upload, amenities  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Set the maximum guest capacity, upload your room photos — try to use high quality pictures, they really make a difference — and then add your amenities like Pool, AC, WiFi, and so on."

**📺 Action:** Click Save  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Hit Save, and your room is live on the website!"

---

### Scene 3E: Settings

**📺 Action:** Click on Settings tab  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Last tab is Settings. This is where you configure your payment methods."

**📺 Action:** Show payment method setup  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Upload your GCash or Bank Transfer QR code here. Add your account name and number so guests know where they're sending money."

**📺 Action:** Show deposit settings  
**🎤 Say:**
> "You can also set up deposits — either a fixed amount or a percentage. This means guests can pay just the deposit to secure their booking and pay the rest when they arrive."

**📺 Action:** Show admin passcode section  
**🎤 Say:**
> "And down here is where you change your admin passcode. Keep this secure and only share it with people you trust."

### Scene 4B: Editing Content

**📺 Action:** Click on a text element on the page  
**🎤 Say:**
> "To change any text — just click on it directly. See? I can edit the headline, the description, anything."

**📺 Action:** Click on an image  
**🎤 Say:**
> "Same with images — click on them to replace with your own photos."

**📺 Action:** Show the color/theme picker  
**🎤 Say:**

**🎤 Say:**
> "Don't forget to hit Save when you're done! All your changes go live instantly."

## 🎯 Pro Tips for a Great Video

1. **Start with the guest side** — your client needs to understand what their customers see FIRST
2. **Then show the admin** — this is the "behind the scenes" that they'll use daily
3. **End with the builder** — this is the "fun part" where they make it their own
4. **Use real data** — fill in realistic room names and prices, not "Test Room 1"
5. **Record in one take per section** — if you mess up, just redo that section
6. **Upload as UNLISTED on YouTube** — only people with the link can see it
7. **Share the link via Messenger** — easy for your clients to access anytime
